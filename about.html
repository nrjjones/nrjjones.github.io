About me

I have spent most of my career conducting population health research and providing methodological consultation to faculty, researchers, and students. I am currently a Senior Project Director at the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. My formal training is in demography and I have been a member of the Population Association of America since 1997. Before moving to Wisconsin, I worked in the CDC’s Office on Smoking and Health, UCLA’s California Center for Population Research, and The RAND Corporation.
